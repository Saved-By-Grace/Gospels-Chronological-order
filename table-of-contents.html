Timeline of Jesus' Ministry

Event Description
The Birth of Jesus Jesus is born in Bethlehem.
The First 1.5 Years of Jesus' Ministry Year of Preparation, beginning Jesus' work in a less public way.
The 2nd Year of Jesus' Ministry Year of Popularity, where Jesus' teachings and miracles gained widespread attention.
The 3rd Year of Jesus' Ministry Year of Opposition, where resistance to Jesus' message and actions grew.
The Last Week of Jesus' Ministry Includes Jesus' entry into Jerusalem, the Last Supper, crucifixion, and resurrection.
40 Days from the Resurrection Jesus appears to His disciples before His ascension into heaven.
